Frequently Asked Questions About Acrylic Nails in Springfield, MA

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails typically last about 2–3 weeks before needing a fill, depending on nail growth and aftercare. Many salons in Springfield offer fill-in services to maintain your set.

What is the difference between a full set and a fill-in?

A full set is a new acrylic application. A fill-in maintains the set by filling new nail growth near the cuticle, helping your acrylics look consistent and last longer.

Are acrylic nails safe for natural nails?

When applied and removed properly by a trained technician, acrylic nails can be worn safely. Avoid peeling them off and follow aftercare recommendations to help protect natural nails.

Do I need an appointment for acrylic nails in Springfield?

Some salons accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during evenings and weekends—especially for longer services, specific shapes, or custom nail designs.
