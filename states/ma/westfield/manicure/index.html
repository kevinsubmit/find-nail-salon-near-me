Frequently Asked Questions About Manicures in Westfield, MA

How much does a manicure cost?

Pricing varies by salon and service type. Gel manicures and spa add-ons usually cost more than a basic manicure.

Do nail salons accept walk-ins?

Many accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended on evenings and weekends.

What’s the difference between gel and regular polish?

Gel polish is cured under a lamp and typically lasts longer with a shinier finish.

How often should I get a manicure?

Many people get manicures every 1–2 weeks, depending on lifestyle and nail growth.
