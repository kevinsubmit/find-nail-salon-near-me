Frequently Asked Questions About Acrylic Nails in Westfield, MA

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails typically last about 2–3 weeks before needing a fill, depending on nail growth and aftercare. Many nail salons offer fill-in services to keep your set looking fresh.

What is the difference between a full set and a fill-in?

A full set is a brand-new acrylic application. A fill-in is maintenance where new growth near the cuticle is filled so your acrylics look consistent and last longer.

Are acrylic nails safe for natural nails?

When applied and removed properly by a trained technician, acrylic nails can be worn safely. Avoid peeling them off yourself and follow aftercare recommendations to help protect natural nails.

Do I need an appointment for acrylic nails in Westfield?

Some salons accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during busy times (evenings and weekends), especially if you want a specific shape, length, or custom design.
