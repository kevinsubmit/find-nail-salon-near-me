Frequently Asked Questions About Acrylic Nails in Westfield, MA

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails often last about 2–3 weeks before needing a fill, depending on nail growth and aftercare.

What’s the difference between a full set and a fill-in?

A full set is a brand-new acrylic application. A fill-in maintains your set by filling new nail growth near the cuticle.

Are acrylic nails safe for natural nails?

When applied and removed properly by a trained technician, acrylics can be worn safely. Avoid peeling them off.

Do I need an appointment?

Some salons accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during evenings and weekends.
