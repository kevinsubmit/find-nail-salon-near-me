Frequently Asked Questions About Gel Nails in Westfield, MA

How long do gel nails last?

Gel polish commonly lasts around 2 weeks with proper prep and aftercare.

Gel vs acrylic — what’s the difference?

Gel polish is a coating cured under a lamp. Acrylic is a stronger enhancement built with powder and liquid.

Can I remove gel polish at home?

Some gels can be soaked off, but professional removal helps reduce damage from over-filing or peeling.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins may be available, but booking ahead is best during peak hours.
