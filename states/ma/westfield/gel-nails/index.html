Frequently Asked Questions About Gel Nails in Westfield, MA

What are gel nails?

Gel nails typically use gel-based polish or extensions that are cured under a UV/LED lamp. They are popular for a glossy finish and better chip resistance compared with regular polish.

How long do gel nails last?

Gel polish commonly lasts around 2 weeks. Gel extensions may last longer depending on nail growth, maintenance, and daily activities.

How should gel nails be removed?

Gel polish should be removed properly (often by soaking and gentle removal). Avoid peeling gel off, because it can damage the surface of natural nails.

Do I need an appointment for gel nails in Westfield?

Some salons accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during evenings and weekends, especially for gel extensions or custom gel nail art.
