Frequently Asked Questions

How long do gel nails last?

Gel polish commonly lasts around 2 weeks. Gel extensions can last longer with proper maintenance.

Are gel nails the same as acrylic nails?

No—gel and acrylic use different materials. Gel is often lighter and cured with a UV/LED lamp.

Can gel nails damage natural nails?

Damage usually comes from improper removal. Ask for safe soak-off removal and avoid peeling gel off.

Do I need an appointment for gel nails in Chicopee?

Appointments are recommended during peak times, especially for extensions or detailed nail art.
