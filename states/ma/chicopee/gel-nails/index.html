Frequently Asked Questions About Gel Nails in Chicopee, MA

How long do gel nails last?

Gel polish typically lasts around 2 weeks with proper prep and aftercare.

Gel vs acrylic — what’s the difference?

Gel polish is cured under a lamp. Acrylic is a stronger enhancement built with powder and liquid.

Can I remove gel at home?

Some gel can be soaked off, but professional removal can help prevent nail damage.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins may be available, but booking ahead is best during peak times.
