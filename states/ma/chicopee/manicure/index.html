Frequently Asked Questions About Manicures in Chicopee, MA

How much does a manicure cost?

Prices vary by salon and service type. Gel manicures and spa add-ons usually cost more.

Do salons accept walk-ins?

Many accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during peak hours.

Gel vs regular polish?

Gel polish is cured under a lamp and often lasts longer with a glossy finish.

How often should I get a manicure?

Many people book every 1–2 weeks depending on lifestyle and nail growth.
