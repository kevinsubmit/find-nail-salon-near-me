Frequently Asked Questions

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails typically last about 2–3 weeks before needing a fill, depending on nail growth and aftercare.

What is the difference between a full set and a fill-in?

A full set is a new acrylic application. A fill-in maintains the set by filling new nail growth near the cuticle.

Are acrylic nails safe for natural nails?

When applied and removed properly by a trained technician, acrylic nails can be worn safely. Avoid peeling them off.

Do I need an appointment for acrylic nails in Chicopee?

Some salons accept walk-ins, but appointments are recommended during evenings and weekends—especially for nail art or longer services.
