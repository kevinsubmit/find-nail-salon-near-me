Frequently Asked Questions About Acrylic Nails in Chicopee, MA

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails often last about 2–3 weeks before needing a fill.

Full set vs fill-in?

A full set is a new application. A fill-in maintains the set by filling new growth near the cuticle.

Are acrylics safe?

With proper application and removal, acrylics can be worn safely. Avoid peeling them off.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins may be available, but appointments are recommended on evenings and weekends.
