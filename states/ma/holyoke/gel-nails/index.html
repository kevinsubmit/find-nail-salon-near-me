Frequently Asked Questions About Gel Nails in Holyoke, MA

How long do gel nails last?

Gel nails often last around 2–3 weeks, depending on nail growth and daily activities.

Are gel nails better than regular polish?

Gel polish typically lasts longer and resists chipping better than regular polish, with a glossy finish.

Do gel nails damage natural nails?

Gel can be safe when applied and removed properly. Avoid peeling gel off—ask a salon to remove it correctly.

Should I book an appointment for gel nails?

Walk-ins may be available, but booking ahead is recommended on busy days—especially for gel extensions or nail art.
